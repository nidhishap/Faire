What we want to achieve

We at Faire believe that being organised is a stepping stone to success. With Faire, we aim to create a neat planner where you can write the tasks you plan to achieve, and keep note of the tasks you have accomplished. Note categories of tasks to be accomplished, groceries to be bought or places to visit, easily with Faire. Remember, there is no such thing as a meaningless task!

FAQs

Q1. What is the point of a planner such as this?
ans. A planner such as this helps you neatly organise tasks into categories and navigate through your tasks, helping you plan early and achieving your goals!

Q2. What does the left and right pannels for adding tasks do?
ans. The left pannel, i.e. The '+' on the left side helps you add broader, eg. groceries, books to buy, homework etc. and after clicking on one of the categories you can click the '+' on the right pannel to add specific tasks to those categories.

Q3. Will my list get lost if I close the tab?
ans. Do not worry, your tasks remain intact even after you have closed your tab.
